Role

UI/UX Designer

3 Months (Q1 2026)

Figma, Photoshop, Canva

Industry

Ecology / Government Portal

The Problem

Citizens and environmental volunteers have limited visibility into real-time health metrics of the Ganges river basins, sewerage treatment plant (STP) statistics, and upcoming ecological campaigns. Muted public engagement slows river conservation efforts.

Existing environmental portals are outdated, cluttered, and do not provide clean pathways for citizens to join local clean-up drives.

Business Goal

Design a visually compelling, clean ecological web portal displaying real-time water purity indices (pH index, BOD levels) and upcoming clean-up drives.

Boost volunteer registration numbers by 45% and establish transparency in sewerage treatment plant operations.

The Solution

We designed the Namami Gange Ecology Portal, a premium web layout displaying water purity scores with clean colors. The interface houses volunteer drive registration forms, water audits records, maps tracking sewerage treatment programs, and direct contribution links.

Retrospective

Challenges, Solutions & Key Learnings

The Challenge

Presenting scientific indicators (BOD, COD, pH levels) to everyday citizens. Audits showed citizens did not understand complex metrics, leading to drop-offs.

The Solution

We designed a color-coded 'River Health Index Gauge' showing simple categories like Good, Moderate, and Poor alongside tooltip definitions. This increased user session duration by 50%.

Key Takeaway

Translating scientific data into human-centric visuals is key to public engagement. Simplicity combined with interactive education details empowers users to support environmental initiatives.
