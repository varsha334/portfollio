The Problem

Industrial grain processing plants operate on complex 3-shift rotations. The plant's manual attendance registers and legacy payroll programs caused frequent errors, delaying employee leaves verification and pay slip distribution.

Employees had to physically visit the HR cabin to request leave status, review shift logs, or generate slip copies, creating administrative bottlenecks.

Business Goal

Centralize shift schedules, leaves tracking, and pay slips into a unified, secure dashboard.

Decrease administrative response times by 60%, eliminate payroll slip printing overhead, and ensure transparency in daily attendance logging.

The Solution

We built Dalmia Grain HRMS, a responsive web dashboard designed for admin managers and field plant workers. The interface displays active rosters, leave approval lists, a secure salary slip generator, and real-time attendance index charts with simple status labels.

Retrospective

Challenges, Solutions & Key Learnings

The Challenge

Admins struggled to edit shift schedules on desktop tables. Drag-and-drop actions caused layout misalignments on touch laptops, resulting in scheduling errors.

The Solution

We designed a 'double-click configuration modal' combined with clear undo/redo buttons. A side conflict indicator alerts admins instantly if a worker is scheduled for double shifts.

Key Takeaway

Enterprise SaaS design must prioritize data density and system safety controls. When design prevents human mistakes (like scheduling overlaps), it drives measurable business cost savings.
