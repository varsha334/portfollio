OLAM Agri Logistics App

Role

Lead Mobile UI/UX Designer

Duration

3 Months (Q4 2025)

Tools

Figma, Illustrator, Miro

Industry

Logistics / Commodity Trading

The Problem

Grain cargo transport operates in highly dynamic supply chain environments. Drivers and logistics coordinators spend hours checking transport schedules, route optimization, and bid offers via manual calls or paper manifests.

Lack of instant bidding transparency and real-time cargo status notifications leads to vehicle idle times and lost revenue for cargo fleets.

Business Goal

Build an intuitive, responsive mobile application for truck drivers and port logistics managers that simplifies cargo bidding and real-time freight tracking.

Reduce coordination delays by 35%, optimize cargo allocations, and secure live telemetry updates from vehicles during transit.

The Solution

We designed the OLAM Agri Logistics Mobile App. The layout features live commodity bidding cards, interactive route GPS mapping coordinates, instant auction alert notifications, and simplified log templates for drivers on the road.

Retrospective

Challenges, Solutions & Key Learnings

The Challenge

Drivers found it difficult to place bids while driving. The number pad inputs were too small and prone to typo errors, causing bid placement frustrations.

The Solution

We designed a single swipe-action 'Quick Bid' slider configured with preset rates. This eliminated typing forms, allowing safe single-tap confirmations at rest stops.

Key Takeaway

Designing interface systems for transit environments requires robust safety constraints and simplified click paths. Reducing input complexities directly drives real-world user adoption and retention.
