Dalmia Grower App

Role

Lead UI/UX Designer

Duration

4 Months (Q2 2025)

Tools

Figma, Adobe Photoshop, Canva

Industry

AgriTech / Enterprise Mobile

The Problem

Sugarcane and grain growers face massive information asymmetry: monitoring soil parameters, diagnosing crop leaf infections in real-time, and recording log books manually. Lack of immediate agronomy support results in a 15% decrease in annual yield efficiency.

Existing enterprise apps are too complex for rural growers, featuring high cognitive load, small touch targets, and complex text interfaces.

Business Goal

Build a localized mobile utility app that simplifies complex agronomy data (moisture, yield metrics, leaf diagnosis) into high-contrast visual tiles.

Empower growers to identify leaf diseases instantly using an AI-based scanner, reducing crop diagnostic feedback time from days to under 10 seconds.

The Solution

We designed a high-contrast dark interface featuring large card layouts, intuitive icons, and color-coded statuses. The application contains an easy-to-use crop health scanner, interactive soil index trackers, and simplified log entries that require minimal typing.

Retrospective

Challenges, Solutions & Key Learnings

The Challenge

Farmers found it difficult to scan leaves under bright sunlight due to screen glare and shadows, resulting in poor image alignment and scanner errors.

The Solution

We designed a smart UI overlay box with high-contrast guides, auto-exposure detection, and an automatic audio prompt instructing farmers how to position the leaf to avoid glare.

Key Takeaway

Enterprise tools for field workers require testing in their direct environment. Designing for outdoor glare, physical dirt, and rural network delays helped us build a highly resilient agronomy mobile platform.
